The code of "RTPP.m" is used to track the mice trajectray in real time during 
real time place preference test (Fig 3).

## instructions

1. Make sure your version of MATLAB meets MATLAB2016a.

2. Click the "save" button and write filename.

3. Click the "Get RefImg" button.

4. Conduct the mouse into the chamber.

5. Click the "start" button.

6. Click the "stop" button when you finished the experiment.

If you use this code in your paper, please cite
Li et al. Hypothalamic Circuits for Predation and Evasion[J]. Neuron, 2018:911.
