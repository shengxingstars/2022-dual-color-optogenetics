The code of "PSTH_DAs.m" is used to analyze the fiber photometry data in Fig 4. "PSTH_DAs.m" is the main function.

## instructions

1. Click the "load" button behind "Stimulus1" to load event data.

2. Click the "load" button behind "Response" to load fluorescence signal.

3. Adjust the parameters on the GUI according to your need. 

4. Click the "Average" button to plot figure.
