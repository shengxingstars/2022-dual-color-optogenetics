The code of "PSTH_spike.m" is used to analyze the In vivo extracellular electrophysiology data in Fig S11. "PSTH_spike.m"  is the main funtion.

## instructions

1. Click the "load" button behind "Stim" to load event data.

2. Click the "load" button behind "Resp" to load spike data.

3. Adjust the parameters on the GUI according to your need.

4. click the "PETH" button to plot figure.
